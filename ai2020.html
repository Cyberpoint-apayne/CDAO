2020 Department of Defense Artificial Intelligence Symposium and Exposition
September 9 & 10, 2020

The DoD’s first ever Artificial Intelligence Symposium and Exposition was held on September 9 and 10, 2020. The two-day virtual event brought together more than 2300 DoD, industry, academia, and across government AI experts and professionals to discuss the Department’s current activities, future direction, challenges, and areas of collaboration with industry and academia that surround the operationalization of AI.


To view some of the videos from the Symposium, please visit our YouTube channel at: https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCvZMJwDUhF_cK366F2ikcXA
