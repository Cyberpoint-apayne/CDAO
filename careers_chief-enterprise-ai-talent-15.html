Strategy and Policy Directorate

Chief of Enterprise AI Talent, GG15

Overview:

This is not your traditional HR role. As the Department of Defense moves aggressively to adopt artificial intelligence at scale, we need a highly skilled, educated, and trained workforce to lead and drive the Department’s digital transformation agenda. JAIC’s new Chief of Enterprise Talent will deliver this workforce on behalf of America’s service members and civilian personnel.

This position demands every ounce your leadership and influence skills, business acumen, and creativity. You will manage cultural change, and drive new requirements for the Department’s existing workforce.

Key Metrics of Success:

  • DoD-wide implementation of an AI Strategic Human Capital Plan that you create.
  • Establishment of an AI Functional Community for civilian and military personnel education and training.
  • Delivery of comprehensive AI education and training strategy and plan.
  • Establish training objectives by work roles, ensuring all AI and related course offerings are included.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Manage the development and implementation of an AI Strategic Human Capital Plan.
  • Plan, direct, and coordinate a variety of human capital projects and assignments.
  • Establish education and training strategies, plans, and accreditation standards for AI curricula.
  • Formulate and implement project plans, policies, and guidelines for AI training objectives and goals.
  • Manage and plan technical training objectives by work roles, ensuring all AI course offerings are included.
  • Establish partnerships with other DoD agencies, state agencies, academia, and industry to ensure the JAIC stays current in human capital practices.
  • Represent the JAIC in Government wide meetings and conferences on Human Capital Strategy related topics.
  • Formulate policies and guidance for effective management.
  • Manage and set priority of projects, and establish timeframe for project completion.
  • Evaluate effectiveness of quality standards to meet mission requirements.
  • Present briefings and presentations, provide guidance, and justify decisions to upper management and executives.

Key Qualifications:

  • Strong ability to cope with ambiguity and navigate complex organizational dynamics.
  • Business or government leadership experience developing and implementing a human capital management program and policies for a new emerging technology mission.
  • Experience leading a robust workforce planning initiative across a large organization that consisted of baseline, future state, gap analysis, and strategy development.
  • Strong knowledge of human capital practices, principles, laws, and policies.
  • Strong knowledge of human capital techniques, trends, and emerging technology.

How To Apply:

Please submit a detailed application package (cover letter/statement of interest, resume, and unofficial transcripts) and inquires to the JAIC Hiring Box at: osd.JAIC-Hiring@mail.mil
