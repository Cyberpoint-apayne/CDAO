MISSION INITIATIVES

Joint Force Protection (Formerly TRP)

Why this MI?

Joint Force Protection (JFP) aims to use AI to transform the joint mission areas aligned against unpredictable threats – missions like Humanitarian Assistance & Disaster Relief, Countering Weapons of Mass Destruction, and Force Protection. JFP will advocate for the adoption of AI to save lives and make the Joint Force more resilient.

Goals

Leverage AI to reduce timelines associated with discovery, resource allocation, and enhanced situational awareness to improve humanitarian aid and disaster relief (HADR) outcomes. Accelerate the adoption of AI across the countering weapons of mass destruction (CWMD) enterprise in order to better prevent, mitigate, and recover from WMD events. Promote AI maturity and adoption across the FP enterprise by identifying critical use cases and producing scalable pipelines and solutions that reduce operator burden and improve FP outcomes.

Example Success Story

(Source: DVIDS)

The HADR team conducted a deliberate user experience assessment with maritime surveillance operators (MSOs) for USCG to ensure the AI tools designed to improve detection synchronized with the existing workflows of the aircrews monitoring airborne sensors. The proof of concept will inform the USCG on hardware requirements to run CV models at the edge, evaluate off the shelf model performance, & inform the team on how to leverage synthetic data to improve maritime detection models.
