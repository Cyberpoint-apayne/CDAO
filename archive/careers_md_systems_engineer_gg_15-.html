Missions Directorate

Systems Engineer, GG-15

INTRODUCTION

The Joint Artificial Intelligence Center (JAIC) was established to enable the Military Departments and Services, Joint Staff, Combatant Commands (CCMDs), Office of the Secretary of Defense (OSD), and other DoD Components, to serve on a Cross-Functional Team (CFT) to swiftly introduce new capabilities and effectively experiment with new operational concepts in support of DoD's warfighting missions and business functions with the overarching goal of accelerating the delivery of AI-enabled capabilities, scaling the Department-wide impact of AI, and synchronizing DoD AI activities to expand Joint Force advantages. A CFT is an inter-disciplinary team including military officers, acquisition officials, and technical experts.

POSITION SUMMARY

The JAIC is looking for a Senior Systems Engineer to join the Missions Directorate. In this role, you will lead a talented team of engineers that demonstrate superb technical competency, delivering mission critical infrastructure and ensuring the highest levels of availability, performance and security. As the senior leader you will be responsible for planning, organizing, developing, engineering, and operationalizing AI-based product solutions.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Lead as a widely recognized technical advisor and authority in DoD critical Artificial Intelligence applications and computing systems design.
  • Provide senior level technical direction, and guidance in the accomplishment of projects including the operational use of AI (both ML and non-ML).
  • Define and initiate tasks in areas where no engineering or AI precedence exists.
  • Adjust task direction and scope to accommodate changes in technical and schedule requirements.
  • Develop new concepts and ideas for achieving goals, technological trends, significant advancements in operational and mission requirements.
  • Lead the development team to engineer overall design concepts and criteria, and reviews and assesses the overall progress in the development effort.
  • Develop methods to assess the end-to-end system performance using operational measurements or other available test beds.
  • Formulate and quantify measures of effectiveness as a basis for comparing product and system configuration alternatives.
  • Lead and advise formulating technical approaches, selection of the tools, diagnostics methods for solving the customer problems utilizing artificial intelligence in a timely manner.
  • Visit field installations, research and development or commercial organizations to obtain or exchange complex technical information.
  • Ensure all the functional and performance analysis models developed reflect the operational performance requirements.
  • Performs other duties as assigned.

POSITION REQUIREMENTS

  • Must be a US Citizen.
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a security clearance.
  • Bachelor’s degree in Engineering or Computer Science.
  • Solid knowledge of artificial intelligence design and performance analysis models, algorithms and tools.
  • Ability to communicate effectively, as an expert spokes person for the agency in all areas of expertise.
  • Proven knowledge of related scientific and engineering fields in order to evaluate design and performance assessment projects from a broad perspective.
  • Solid experience in software intensive systems, including the architecting of systems, and human factors engineering.
  • Must be a recognized expert in artificial intelligence with comprehensive knowledge of the computer and information systems and their interrelationships.
  • Proven experience in concepts, theories, practices and techniques of engineering for information systems.
  • A bachelor’s degree in computer science (1550) or engineering (0854) (*See Education Requirements Fact Sheet)

TO APPLY

Please submit a detailed application package (cover letter/statement of interest, resume, and unofficial transcripts) and inquires to the JAIC Hiring Box at: osd.JAIC-Hiring@mail.mil
