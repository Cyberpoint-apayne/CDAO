Products Directorate

Data Quality Engineer,  GG-15

INTRODUCTION

The Joint Artificial Intelligence Center (JAIC) was established to enable the Military Departments and Services, Joint Staff, Combatant Commands (CCMDs), Office of the Secretary of Defense (OSD), and other DoD Components, to serve on a Cross-Functional Team (CFT) to swiftly introduce new capabilities and effectively experiment with new operational concepts in support of DoD's warfighting missions and business functions with the overarching goal of accelerating the delivery of AI-enabled capabilities, scaling the Department-wide impact of AI, and synchronizing DoD AI activities to expand Joint Force advantages. A CFT is an inter-disciplinary team including military officers, acquisition officials, and technical experts.

POSITION SUMMARY

The JAIC is looking for a Data Quality Engineer to provide test data support management and oversight of test data. You will also be responsible for a broad range of Data Engineering analyses entailing unique problems, creativity, innovative use of techniques, and advance approaches. Additionally providing testing, evaluation and assessment expertise and support to improve verification and validation that will accelerate the use of AI technology for the DoD.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Develop and document operational concepts and strategies for assigned projects to improve data quality and volume.
  • Accelerate iteration cycles for personnel using data in DoD.
  • Engineer data sets that include data across multiple sources, and the correct aggregation, processing, labeling, and tagging.
  • Provide manipulation to support algorithm development, test and assessment, evaluation, machine learning, and fielding.
  • Collaborate with colleagues to analyze AI products and deploy AI T&E pipelines.
  • Lead efforts to standardize AI data ingestion.
  • Review project test plans, test schedule, resource needs.
  • Provide expertise in data and information management specific to testing and evaluation of AI systems.
  • Separate data into training, validation, and test data sets.
  • Develop technical standards, principles, theories, concepts, and techniques to synthesize customer requirements and design the data ingestion, curation, and T&E pipeline for AI products.
  • Provide expertise in data support to upper management and oversight of data and access to test data.
  • Provide expertise in data and information management specific to testing and evaluation of AI systems.

POSITION REQUIREMENTS

  • Must be a US Citizen with ability to possess and maintain a security clearance.
  • Leads and/or writes production-level, re-usable, interpretable code in Python, C++, SQL, or other languages using Test Driven Development to create T&E pipelines, test harnesses, data curation, and visualization code.
  • Proven understanding of optimization/search algorithms, unsupervised learning algorithms, supervised learning, neural networks, and other regression and classification methodologies.
  • Advanced professional experience in engineering/computer theories, principles, concepts, standards, and methods related to data engineering.
  • Proven experience in solving unique data problems related to the development of AI products and tools.
