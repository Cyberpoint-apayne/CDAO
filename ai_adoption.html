Introduction

The Department of Defense is at a transformational moment in history. AI is rewriting almost every aspect of business and society. Data, predictive analytics and AI are powering financial systems, production industries, business-decision-making, retail markets, social media, and services delivery. The DoD must do the same thing. We must adopt AI to transform how we operate and fight as a Joint Force.

This is not easy. Soldiers, Sailors, Airmen, Marines and Guardians must think differently about their challenges, and find proven, data-enabled and machine-teaming solutions. We wants to help those who are thinking about using AI along their “AI Adoption Journey.” The suite of videos, documents, and other information is yours to explore, and will help you map out the critical milestones along your journey to modern and relevant AI-enabled capabilities.

To successfully adopt AI, you need to understand it – or at least understand the parts that are critical for your role. The Training and Education team is delivering training programs to provide every level of the DoD Workforce with knowledge that will help them be AI-Ready. Whether you are Leading, Driving, Creating, Embedding, Facilitating, or Employing AI, there are training opportunities for you.
