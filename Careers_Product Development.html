Capability Delivery Directorate

Product Development
GG-0854/1550/1529-15

INTRODUCTION

The Joint Artificial Intelligence Center (JAIC) was established to enable the Military Departments and Services, Joint Staff, Combatant Commands (CCMDs), Office of the Secretary of Defense (OSD), and other DoD Components, to serve on a Cross-Functional Team (CFT) to swiftly introduce new capabilities and effectively experiment with new operational concepts in support of DoD's warfighting missions and business functions with the overarching goal of accelerating the delivery of AI-enabled capabilities, scaling the Department-wide impact of AI, and synchronizing DoD AI activities to expand Joint Force advantages. A CFT is an inter-disciplinary team including military officers, acquisition officials, and technical experts.

POSITION SUMMARY

This position serves as a recognized technical authority and senior advisor to the Mission Initiative (MI) Cross-Functional Teams in the JAIC Capability Delivery Directorate. Provides technical guidance and advisement to solve major problems in systems architecture and software development. Leverages an understanding of enterprise software development practices, including Agile software development methods to deliver capability for DoD end users. Applies comprehensive knowledge of computer software and hardware concepts (such as data structures, operating systems, systems architecture, and control systems) to provide technical advisement on the development of integrated computer systems; and the adequacy and suitability of test plans, designs, and requirements. Ensures the efficacy of proper test design to facilitate the full evaluation of system/equipment performance against defined user requirements. Serves as a consultant on the planning, designing, and review of designs for software systems that include artificial intelligence/machine learning technology and tools. Provides advice on the architecture, design, and software development initiatives for software intensive systems, including the development and approval processes for system engineering plans, and technology readiness assessments. Establishes strategic partnerships with internal and external stakeholders to exchange best practices and lessons learned in emerging software development technologies, tools, and practices. Develops and deploys strategies for end user engagement and collaboration across DoD mission areas.

POSITION REQUIREMENTS

A bachelor’s degree in computer science (1550), engineering (0854), mathematics (1529), or statistics (1529) (*See Education Requirements Fact Sheet)

Mastery of a specialty field to the extent that the engineer is capable of applying new developments and experienced judgment to solve novel or obscure problems; ability to extend and modify existing techniques; and ability to develop new approaches for use of other engineering specialists in solving a variety of engineering problems.

Mastery of and skill in applying expertise in advanced professional engineering/computer/mathematical theories, principles, concepts, standards, and methods sufficient to serve as a senior advisor regarding new applications to resolve unique problems, conditions, or issues related to product development.

c

In-depth knowledge of advanced principles and practices of modeling and simulation as well as engineering requirements and methods, in order to investigate and provide expert technical advice on all aspects of science and technology being developed for DoD applications.

Mastery knowledge and understanding of DoD Policies and Federal Law and Regulation (public law, Executive Orders, Presidential Directives and Federal regulations) governing national security subjects related to artificial intelligence goals and objectives.

Demonstrated ability to work with senior staff of all Department of Defense Components, the Intelligence Community, and other Federal agencies.

Skill in oral and written communication sufficient to maintain liaison and correspondence with engineers in other organizations who are expert consultants on processes, trends and innovations; and to advise and consult with upper management.

CONDITIONS OF EMPLOYMENT

If education is a requirement for the position, applicants must provide a copy of their unofficial transcript(s) from their degree awarding institution(s), along with a resume.

Applicants must be able to obtain and maintain the appropriate security clearance levels for this position. Applicants may be subject to initial and random drug testing for this position, if applicable.

ADDITONAL INFORMATION

This is a Cyber Excepted Service (CES) Position: Work Category (Professional); Work Level (Expert/3)
