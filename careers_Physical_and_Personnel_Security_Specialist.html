Front Office Directorate

Physical and Personnel Security Specialist
GG-0080-14

INTRODUCTION

The Joint Artificial Intelligence Center (JAIC) was established to enable the Military Departments and Services, Joint Staff, Combatant Commands (CCMDs), Office of the Secretary of Defense (OSD), and other DoD Components, to serve on a Cross-Functional Team (CFT) to swiftly introduce new capabilities and effectively experiment with new operational concepts in support of DoD's warfighting missions and business functions with the overarching goal of accelerating the delivery of AI- enabled capabilities, scaling the Department-wide impact of AI, and synchronizing DoD AI activities to expand Joint Force advantages. A CFT is an inter-disciplinary team including military officers, acquisition officials, and technical experts.

POSITION SUMMARY

Serves as the Physical and Personnel Security Specialist for the JAIC under the direct supervision of the Chief, Counter and Protect Division and the general supervision of JAIC Deputy Director. The incumbent is a primary point of contact for all security-related programs to protect JAIC’s personnel, sensitive information, activities, technology, and operations against overt/covert acts by criminal elements, dissident groups, terrorists, and foreign intelligence entities. Responsible for the development, management, administration, and implementation of policies and programs to prevent against and mitigate the risk from compromise, unauthorized disclosure, espionage, or sabotage. Participates in various security working groups and boards with Federal, DoD, IC, and Military Service counterparts to remain up-to-date and proficient in current security techniques and technologies and resolve common concerns through standardized guidelines and procedures. Advises JAIC leadership on new developments and advances in security techniques.

POSITION REQUIREMENTS

Mastery knowledge of the security administration field to include thorough knowledge of national, DoD, JCS, and other military department policies, concepts, and regulations pertinent to the multi-disciplined security field in order to: develop new polices and program objectives for a wide range of security concepts and standards to ensure the security integrity of the JAIC; identify and analyze problems; distinguish between relevant and irrelevant information to make logical decisions; provide solutions to JAIC security issues; maintain, review, and monitor program security classification guides (SCG), procedure guides, and OPSEC plans as pertains to JAIC and other agency programs; implement security classification guidance for all JAIC programs; and develop security programs, policies, and procedures in response to emerging technologies and expanded security requirements. Authoritative source of multi-functional security programs for the JAIC.

Uses knowledge of a wide range of security concepts, principles, and practices to review, analyze, and resolve difficult and complex security problems; develop policies, procedures, and planning guidance; and identify those areas, activities, functions, data, or information deemed most important to keep from security threats. The solutions developed often result in new methods, approaches, or procedures.

Knowledge at the expert level of state-of-the-art security technology and equipment. Ability to recommend for acquisition, development or modification, those items and products essential to ensure optimal protection of valuable JAIC resources.

Ability to communicate effectively both orally and in writing to present, defend, document, and/or justify rationale for actions, formulate policy, and to develop clear and coherent policy, instructions, reports and correspondence.

CONDITIONS OF EMPLOYMENT

Applicants must be able to obtain and maintain TS/SCI clearance.

Applicants are subject to initial and random drug testing for this position.

ADDITONAL INFORMATION

This is a Cyber Excepted Service (CES) Position; Work Level (Expert/4)

TO APPLY

Please submit a detailed application package (cover letter/statement of interest, resume, and unofficial transcripts) and inquires to the JAIC Hiring Box at: osd.JAIC-Hiring@mail.mil
