Capability Delivery Directorate

Interdisciplinary Software Developer
GG-0854/1550/14

INTRODUCTION

The Joint Artificial Intelligence Center (JAIC) is established to enable the Military Departments and Services, Joint Staff, Combatant Commands (CCMDs), Office of the Secretary of Defense (OSD), and other DoD Components, to swiftly introduce new capabilities and effectively experiment with new operational concepts in support of DoD's warfighting missions and business functions with the overarching goal of accelerating the delivery of AI enabled capabilities, scaling the Department-wide impact of AI, and synchronizing DoD AI activities to expand Joint Force advantages.

POSITION SUMMARY

As senior software developer, performs systems design and programming necessary to develop, maintain, modify and enhance applications software required to support National Mission Initiatives and Component Mission Initiatives (NMI/CMI) and other stakeholder’s programmatic and administrative systems. Performs testing, implementation and maintenance activities for projects or major aspects of projects assigned. Provides program design and coding specifications for implementation, including: software development strategy, and descriptions of data and file organizations.

Plans, develops, schedules, and tests AI/ML software applications in support of JAIC NMI, CMI, and other stakeholder missions using established standards and agile system lifecycles. Provides technical guidance during a significant project in designing, coding, testing, debugging, and maintaining programs; and translating and interpreting functional requirements. Applies computer assisted software engineering (CASE) tools to the design and development process. Tests, installs, implements, documents and maintains software during a significant local project or as needed, to support applications which have a broad impact.

Responsible for developing new or maintaining current, software systems. Assesses the performance of existing systems against future mission requirements; determines the need for hardware/software upgrades or initiation of new system developments; identifies and determines the criticality of software/firmware and hardware problems associated with computer driven instrumentation and data collection systems; devises solutions to these problems, upgrade capabilities or create software to support customer requirements.

Provides technical knowledge on the application of computer systems engineering methodologies to the design of computer driven systems. Applies professional knowledge of computer systems, including software architecture, memories, bus structures, and interfacing techniques, minicomputers, operating systems, programming languages, data base management systems, computer aided software engineering, and programming techniques.

Identifies technical gaps and weaknesses in the integrated software development process and in ongoing and proposed exploratory and advanced development computer and software projects. The incumbent manages the development of advanced automated tools to support the integration design, development, implementation and management of mission-critical software. Serves as a technical authority regarding the availability and maturity of technology for use in the integrated software development of systems. Plans systems analysis and engineering design efforts for JAIC.

Maintains source code; and modifies and/or upgrades code as necessary during the local phase of a national project or as needed to support applications which have broad impact.

Performs software development, integration, and troubleshooting on a multitude of systems. Conducts analysis of system requirements to establish functional requirements and identify and perform hardware/software trade-offs. Develops conceptual design of computer systems through analysis of computer hardware, interface hardware, software requirements, and network architectures as elements of the total system design. Formulates and conducts hardware and/or software demonstrations to validate design concepts and to provide a basis for full-scale engineering development and subsequent production.

Serves as a team-leader and project coordinator for all software development tasks and provides assistance to, and coordinates the work efforts of others. Provides guidance to less experienced coworkers in solving programming problems.

Establishes, leads, and manages highly technical assessments of JAIC’s functional processes and supporting software systems.

Documents knowledge gaps, risks, and opportunities associated with JAIC functions and recommends both automated and non-automated strategies for knowledge-enabling functional processes across the organization.

Leads the software development process by specifying functional and IT integration requirements in sufficient detail to support design and development. 

Researches emerging standards, tools, and techniques for both assessing and performing software and systems engineering processes and applies them as appropriate.

Performs other related duties as assigned.

POSITION REQUIREMENTS

A bachelor’s degree in engineering (0854) or computer science (1550). (*See Education Requirements Fact Sheet)

Mastery of, and skill in applying the principles of advanced technology computer hardware, MS SQL Server and Oracle DB Server; PHP, Java, Ruby on Rails, and MS .NET application framework; and both Apache and MS IIS web servers and operating systems (including but not limited to Microsoft Server, UNIX, Solaris, LINUX and various Web-server products), computer security, scalable graph visualization, information fusion, stream processing, and related technologies to investigate, advise on, and resolve unusual and controversial problems relating to the interface and integration requirements for a broad range of existing and new electronic information systems and equipment with widely divergent national and international network systems; evaluate and advise on the feasibility of new technology and equipment for incorporation into existing systems; manage, coordinate, direct, and oversee critical operational and integration systems engineering efforts to establish, maintain, and/or improve DoD information systems, DoD analytical tools and data sources, national and international networks, and their hardware and software applications; conduct and advise on testing operations for acceptance and operation of complete systems and equipment, and in all aspects of the project life cycle (feasibility, requirements, design, implementation, integration, testing and deployment); uses expert knowledge of the fundamentals and principles of professional engineering; advanced technology computer hardware, system software, computer systems architecture development and integration; mathematical algorithm structures; electronics theory, interactive graphics equipment and terminals; and principles and techniques of telecommunications to resolve unusual and controversial problems and conflicting systems related issues.

Skill in oral and written communications draft, review, and present correspondence, reports, plans, staff actions, and briefings; and to advise and consult with upper management.

CONDITIONS OF EMPLOYMENT

If education is a requirement for the position, applicants must provide a copy of their unofficial transcript(s) from their degree awarding institution(s), along with a resume.

Applicants must be able to obtain and maintain the appropriate security clearance levels for this position. Applicants may be subject to initial and random drug testing for this position, if applicable.

Positions which perform information security as an inherent, primary purpose of their position: Information Assurance Certification is a condition of employment. This position includes information assurance (IA) work as a paramount duty requirement. Per DoD 8570.01-M, the incumbent of this position must achieve the appropriate IA certification within six months of assignment of these duties. A waiver of this six month requirement may be granted per DoD 8570.01-M, C3.2.4.2 or C3.2.4.3. Failure to receive the proper IA certification may result in removal from this position.

ADDITONAL INFORMATION

This is a Cyber Excepted Service (CES) Position: Work Category (Professional); Work Level (Senior/3)

TO APPLY
