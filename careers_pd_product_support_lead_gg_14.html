Products Directorate

Product Support Lead, GG-14

INTRODUCTION

The Joint Artificial Intelligence Center (JAIC) was established to enable the Military Departments and Services, Joint Staff, Combatant Commands (CCMDs), Office of the Secretary of Defense (OSD), and other DoD Components, to serve on a Cross-Functional Team (CFT) to swiftly introduce new capabilities and effectively experiment with new operational concepts in support of DoD's warfighting missions and business functions with the overarching goal of accelerating the delivery of AI-enabled capabilities, scaling the Department-wide impact of AI, and synchronizing DoD AI activities to expand Joint Force advantages. A CFT is an inter-disciplinary team including military officers, acquisition officials, and technical experts.

POSITION SUMMARY

  • Location: Arlington, VA | USA
  • Job Type: Permanent
  • Salary: $126,233k-164,102k
  • Open & Close Dates: 03/02/2022-03/15/2022
  • Number of Openings: 01

The JAIC is looking for an experienced Product Support Lead to join our Products Directorate. In this position, you will be in charge of developing budgets, operating plans for programs, and writing program funding proposals. The ideal candidate will have a sharp business mind and a proven ability to strategize and implement high level program initiatives.

DUTIES & RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Use technical knowledge of business practices for IT and AI projects to create the structures needed to accelerate information and project accomplishment on projects throughout the JAIC. 
  •  Serve as Product Support of a multidisciplinary team. 
  •  Responsible for the overall management, control, coordination and execution of assigned projects. 
  •  Provides direction and leadership to contract project managers, implement corporate decisions, guidance, laws, regulations, and policy in the management of multiple projects and intermediate prototypes in support of the product. 
  •  Determine the affordability of the life cycle cost estimates of the competing alternatives; including contractor, to select the most promising system/equipment items. 
  •  Negotiate and integrate all specific functions and their requirements from multiple DoD stakeholders, and other agencies/organizations in support of assigned projects. 
  •  Develop and manage the product roadmap. 
  •  Conduct a continuous study of the schedule and performance requirements as they compare to cost as an independent variable. 
  •  Make recommendations to enact changes to the schedule and performance baseline to the client and the appropriate managers as they are determined to contribute to a cost reduction plan. 
  •  Analyze the results of reviews, government and contractor technical reports, and determines the progress and technical adequacy of the project, as compared to an approved project baseline for cost, schedule and performance. 
  •  Exercise centralized accountability and oversight of all product and supporting projects resources. 
  •  Identify expertise needed for and recommend agency employees and contractors to work on a project team of professional and technical specialists typically consisting of system engineers, other matrix support specialists, and contractor support, as required 
  •  Create and manage JAIC AI long term goals. 
  •  Develop a budget and operating plan. 
  •  Scope and prioritize activities based on business and customer impact. 
  •  Manage a team with a diverse array of talents and responsibilities. 
  •  Develop and control deadlines, budgets and activates. 
  •  Meet financial objectives by preparing an annual budget, scheduling expenditures, analyzing variances, and initiating corrective actions. 
  •  Achieve operational objectives by contributing information and recommendations to strategic plans. 
  •  Coordinate with other contractors, subcontractors and vendors to provide overall direction and guidance, track progress and status. 
  •  Prepare and present written communications, plans, briefings and reports to leadership.

QUALIFICATIONS & REQUIREMENTS

  • Extensive management experience to review, analyze and evaluate product development and progress.
  • Mastery knowledge of management concepts, principles, methods and practices, as well as a full range of product management activities starting from Concept and Exploration, and continues through development, production, and completion of initial fielding of the system/equipment items, and transition to another program office for sustainment and operations.
  • Expert knowledge of the principles of Integrated Product Team (IPT) management to conduct product management activities which include: initiating action to identify alternative concepts to satisfy the requirement; tailoring the acquisition strategy to select those concepts for evaluation; working with private industry to identify alternatives that may satisfy the Requirement Document; and selecting those alternatives that meet cost, schedule, and performance objectives.
  • Broad knowledge and skills related to risk management, schedule management, scope management and budget management.
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills along with the ability to plan long term strategies to assess current change impacts.
  • Mastery knowledge of AI technologies a plus.
  • Experience in dealing with complexity, analyzing information, and implementing company vision and mission.
  • Skill in financial planning and strategy
  • Skill in strategic planning
  • Experience in advanced management roles
