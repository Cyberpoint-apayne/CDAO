Products Directorate

Deputy Chief of Products, GG-15, 0301

INTRODUCTION

The Joint Artificial Intelligence Center (JAIC) was established to enable the Military Departments and Services, Joint Staff, Combatant Commands (CCMDs), Office of the Secretary of Defense (OSD), and other DoD Components, to serve on a Cross-Functional Team (CFT) to swiftly introduce new capabilities and effectively experiment with new operational concepts in support of DoD's warfighting missions and business functions with the overarching goal of accelerating the delivery of AI-enabled capabilities, scaling the Department-wide impact of AI, and synchronizing DoD AI activities to expand Joint Force advantages. A CFT is an inter-disciplinary team including military officers, acquisition officials, and technical experts.

POSITION SUMMARY

  • Location: Arlington, VA | USA
  • Job Type: Permanent
  • Salary: $148,484-$176,300k

The JAIC is looking for a Deputy Chief of Products who will be responsible for product planning and execution throughout the full lifecycle of all AI based products. You will join a dynamic and fast-paced environment to develop, design, and implement AI products that deliver the vision, mission and strategy of our organization.

DUTIES & RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Develop methods to test the end-to-end system performance of developed products.
  • Formulate measures of effectiveness as a basis for comparing product and system configuration alternatives.
  • Develop and implement a DoD-wide infrastructure enabling rapid AI product delivery.
  • Plan and assign tasks , approve leave, set and adjust priorities.
  • Recommends approval of contract task manager and project manager selections.
  • Oversee product and project management requirements for target transition systems, prototypes and pilots.
  • Manage the coordination and execution of assigned projects.
  • Translate product strategy into detailed requirements and prototypes.
  • Create product roadmaps.
  • Work closely with engineering teams to deliver quick time to market and optimal resources.
  • Scope and prioritize activities based on business and customer impact.
  • Extensive management experience to review, analyze and evaluate product development and progress.
  • Introduce and market new products by developing time integrated plans with sales and engineering.
  • Prepares briefs and defends systems decision papers or other decision documents.
