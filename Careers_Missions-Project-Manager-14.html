Capability Delivery Directorate

Missions Project Manager
GG-0854/1550-14

INTRODUCTION

The Joint Artificial Intelligence Center (JAIC) was established to enable the Military Departments and Services, Joint Staff, Combatant Commands (CCMDs), Office of the Secretary of Defense (OSD), and other DoD Components, to serve on a Cross-Functional Team (CFT) to swiftly introduce new capabilities and effectively experiment with new operational concepts in support of DoD's warfighting missions and business functions with the overarching goal of accelerating the delivery of AI-enabled capabilities, scaling the Department-wide impact of AI, and synchronizing DoD AI activities to expand Joint Force advantages. A CFT is an inter-disciplinary team including military officers, acquisition officials, and technical experts.

POSITION SUMMARY

Serves as a Project Manager for the Missions Division in the Capability Delivery Directorate. Provides technical guidance, analysis and recommendations to JAIC Teams in order to manage the full spectrum of project planning, implementation and execution of approved Capability Directorate projects. Develops short and long-term project plans; conducts assessments on the feasibility and costs of proposed initiatives and enhancements. Ensures that mission owner requirements are identified to define project scope, milestones, and deliverables. Monitors project schedules, costs, and performance to identify potential risks; and develops appropriate courses of action to mitigate challenges. Conducts continuous technical reviews of tasks and deliverables to ensure the maximum efficiency, economy, and effectiveness of the operations across each project. Continually evaluates the allocation and usage of resources across various JAIC projects; and makes recommendations to the CFT leader for transferring resources between projects as necessary to ensure efficient and economical management. Supports the Chief, Missions by preparing briefs and decision papers at each milestone or In Process Review (IPR) across the life cycle of the projects.

POSITION REQUIREMENTS

A bachelor’s degree in engineering (0854) or computer science (1550). (*See Education Requirements Fact Sheet)

Comprehensive level knowledge of, and skill in applying advanced theories of computer science and/or engineering, in order to provide technical guidance for programs, projects, and initiatives related to Department of Defense Artificial Intelligence goals and objectives.

Extensive knowledge of project management principles processes and procedures using such best practices as the PMI PMBOK, SEI CMMI, Agile Methodology, and Lean Six Sigma standards.

Vast knowledge of project management principles and practices to include and understanding of technical requirements, acquisition policy, and budgeting.

Comprehensive knowledge of state-of-the-art developments in application programming, design, equipment configuration, and communication principles in order to complete the full scale of project activities. Such projects require consideration of the system's participant's/users in other agencies within and outside DoD, private businesses such as the commercial transportation industry, and contracting officers.

Knowledge of systems development life cycle (including expertise in system requirements, design, test, and implementation) in order to review and assess the impact of new systems design policies on projects and programs.

Knowledge of system design principals, procedures, and processes; in order to effectively integrate elements of system analysis, software development, database administration, data lifecycle management, and product release management.

Ability to communicate effectively, both orally and in writing, with senior officials from a broad array of activities, including the federal governments, and industry.

Skill in planning, organizing, and directing the work of government and contract personnel, and integrating manpower and financial resources into a productive team.

Skill and ability to work through the project management processes to initiate, plan, execute, monitor, control and close projects.

CONDITIONS OF EMPLOYMENT

If education is a requirement for the position, applicants must provide a copy of their unofficial transcript(s) from their degree awarding institution(s), along with a resume.

Applicants must be able to obtain and maintain the appropriate security clearance levels for this position. Applicants may be subject to initial and random drug testing for this position, if applicable.

ADDITONAL INFORMATION

This is a Cyber Excepted Service (CES) Position: Work Category (Professional); Work Level (Senior/3)

TO APPLY

Please submit a detailed application package (cover letter/statement of interest, resume, and unofficial transcripts) and inquires to the JAIC Hiring Box at: osd.JAIC-Hiring@mail.mil
