2021 Department of Defense Artificial Intelligence Symposium and Tech Exchange

2021 Department of Defense Artificial Intelligence Symposium and Tech Exchange

June 22 - 24, 2021

The 2021 DoD AI Symposium and Technology Exchange will virtually bring together thought leaders from across the National Security enterprise to discuss themes critical for transforming the U.S. military through the acceleration and integration of AI. Topics will include: competitive challenges, technological opportunities, Research & Development, Responsible AI, and AI Readiness and Implementation.


ABOUT THE EVENT

2021 Department of Defense Artificial Intelligence Symposium (June 22 and 23)

The 2021 Department of Defense Artificial Intelligence Symposium will connect government officials from those Service, Agency, and OSD organizations that drive the DoD’s AI/ML progress with innovators and leaders from industry, academia, and non-profit research institutions for two days of presentations, discussions, and meetings. During this time, there will also be an Exposition area for industry, academia, and government organizations to highlight their AI capabilities.

To register to attend the 2021 DoD AI Symposium and Tech Exchange, or to host a virtual booth, click here.


DoD AI Tech Exchange - Industry and Academia Presentations (June 24)

The DoD AI Tech Exchange (June 24) will showcase cutting-edge, innovative AI research and development that is being developed in industry and academia in support of our nation’s defense. Industry and Academia presentations will focus on technical tracks including: Data Engineering and Infrastructure; Advances in AI Algorithms; or Testing, Evaluation, and Deployment.

If you are in industry or academia and would like to apply to present an AI capability that your organization is developing, you will need to submit a proposal for the DoD AI Technology Exchange by midnight EST on May 14th. To apply:

  1. Fill out the following form and identify up to three supporting documents.
  2. Email your completed application document to osd.pentagon.dod-cio.mbx.ai-events@mail.mil with the subject line “Tech_Exchange_Speaker_Application” with the subject line “Tech_Exchange_Speaker_Application” (including underscores). Failure to input the correct subject line may cause your application to not be processed.
  3. Decisions will be announced by May 25th, with final presentation materials due by June 2, 2021
