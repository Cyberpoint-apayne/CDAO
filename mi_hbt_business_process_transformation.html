Health and Business Transformation: Business Process Transformation

Enabling Trusted Modern Business Operations for the DoD

Business Process Transformation team focuses on improving existing processes and using AI to automate, improve, and enhance the Department. This has proven to:

  • Shift repetitive, low value tasks to machines to free up people to perform high value work
  • Increase work capacity, reduce error rates, expedite timelines, and avoid unnecessary costs with intelligent automation
  • Enhance discovery, access, and use of essential data for back-office business operations

With our partners, we are focusing on:

  • Business Transformation: Transforming DoD business processes through adoption of AI technologies to increase efficiency while reducing cost
  • Policy & Law: Using Natural Language Processing (NLP) to generate, store, and sort legal documents by identifying connection points while reducing redundancies, and/or inconsistencies between requirements.
  • Finance: Improving accuracy and auditability of financial transactions
