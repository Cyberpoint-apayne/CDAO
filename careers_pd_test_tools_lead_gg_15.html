Products Directorate

Test Tools Lead, GG-15

INTRODUCTION

The Joint Artificial Intelligence Center (JAIC) was established to enable the Military Departments and Services, Joint Staff, Combatant Commands (CCMDs), Office of the Secretary of Defense (OSD), and other DoD Components, to serve on a Cross-Functional Team (CFT) to swiftly introduce new capabilities and effectively experiment with new operational concepts in support of DoD's warfighting missions and business functions with the overarching goal of accelerating the delivery of AI-enabled capabilities, scaling the Department-wide impact of AI, and synchronizing DoD AI activities to expand Joint Force advantages. A CFT is an inter-disciplinary team including military officers, acquisition officials, and technical experts.

POSITION SUMMARY

The JAIC is looking for a Test Tools Lead to provide technical expertise, and act as an advisor in formulating technical tools, diagnostic methods, and reporting process for system evaluation. In this role, you will oversee the development of test tools, test harnesses, and application program interfaces (API) to test AI products.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Lead and plan testing efforts.
  • Develop and execute a monitoring plan to ensure that systems meet suitability, user experience, interoperability, information assurance/security, and performance requirements.
  • Develop testing tools and diagnostic methods to effectively meet test and evaluation requirements.
  • Enhance the information exchange between Product Test Engineers, Project Managers, and End Users.
  • Develop detailed test plans, procedures and techniques, to test facilities and equipment, data collection and processing systems.
  • Provide adequate data on which to base evaluations of all critical aspects of compatibility and interoperability with other systems and customer requirements.
  • Collaborate with colleagues to analyze AI products and deploy AI T&E pipelines.
  • Assist in creating robust, scalable data management and test harnesses that enable thorough evaluation of AI systems.
  • Develop methods to assess the end-to-end systems performance using operational measurements and create production level, scalable software to conduct the analysis.
  • Manage project costs, schedules, and deliverables.

POSITION REQUIREMENTS

  • Must be a US Citizen with ability to possess and maintain a security clearance.
  • Leads and/or writes production-level, re-usable, interpretable code in Python, C++, SQL, or other languages using Test Driven Development to create T&E pipelines, test harnesses, data curation, and visualization code.
  • Proven understanding of optimization/search algorithms, unsupervised learning algorithms, supervised learning, neural networks, and other regression and classification methodologies.
  • Advanced professional experience in engineering/computer theories, principles, concepts, standards, and methods related to data engineering.
  • Proven expertise in applying advanced professional engineering/computer theories, principles, concepts, and standards sufficient to apply new applications or developments.
  • Proven experience related to capability/requirements definitions, test and evaluation planning, developmental testing, operational testing, programming, and budgeting.
  • Proven experience in related scientific and engineering fields in order to evaluate design and performance assessment projects.
  • Experience in natural language processing, computer vision, neural networks, and other regression and classification methodologies.
