Algorithmic wars of the future will employ force across all domains in contested and degraded environments. AI-enabled capabilities will ensure the Department of Defense gains distinct strategic advantages in decision, speed, agility, and strategy.

Joint C2, Maneuver and Fires (JC2MF) integrates AI-enabled applications into warfighting systems, processes, and platforms to provide sustained competitive military advantage to the United States â€“ with the objective to deter adversaries or, if deterrence fails, to fight and win.

With our mission partners, JFP is bringing AI-enabled capabilities to:

  • Command and Control: Provides commanders decision advantage through broadened operational information, autonomy and automation at machine speeds
  • Fires: Seeks to penetrate adversary defenses and defeat adversary attacks through AI-enabled speed, lethality, accuracy, and synchronization
  • Movement and Maneuver: Leverages AI in the planning and execution of force deployments and support the ability of Joint forces to achieve a position of advantage over our adversaries
