Programs, Budget, and Acquisition Directorate

Resource Analyst, GG-14

INTRODUCTION

The Joint Artificial Intelligence Center (JAIC) was established to enable the Military Departments and Services, Joint Staff, Combatant Commands (CCMDs), Office of the Secretary of Defense (OSD), and other DoD Components, to serve on a Cross-Functional Team (CFT) to swiftly introduce new capabilities and effectively experiment with new operational concepts in support of DoD's warfighting missions and business functions with the overarching goal of accelerating the delivery of AI-enabled capabilities, scaling the Department-wide impact of AI, and synchronizing DoD AI activities to expand Joint Force advantages. A CFT is an inter-disciplinary team including military officers, acquisition officials, and technical experts.

POSITION SUMMARY

The JAIC is looking for a Resource Analyst to join the Budget division. In this position, you will perform a broad range of functions relating to planning, programming, budgeting and financial management to assigned projects. You will also develop new ideas, concepts, methods and techniques that will facilitate the development and provision of technical and programmatic guidance to the organization. The ideal candidate has a broad knowledge of systems planning and operational problems, can detects flaws, voids, and other deficiencies in related plans and programs.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Develop, review, and provide advice and recommendations on the activities budgets, acquisition plans, and multi-year programs.
  • Provide expert level advice on all phases of the budget process and financial management.
  • Provide all supporting financial information for inclusion in comprehensive budget exhibits and schedules.
  • Ensures adequate detailed data is available to support budget programs.
  • Analyze budget execution and recommend program adjustments when necessary.
  • Assist with presentation and justification of requests and ensure proper allocation of resulting funds.
  • Serves as financial consultant to management.
  • Submits budgetary reports and written analyses.
  • Represents JAIC in various working groups, senior level meetings, etc.
  • Assist program managers in developing contractual plans.
  • Analyzes Congressional actions concerning the President's biennial Budget as they pertain to the activities, and prepares any required responses when warranted.
  • Participate in development and execution of the current year budget.
  • Develop and coordinate written testimony, support documentation, and justification related to the Budget Estimate and the President's Budget.
  • Analyze technical plans, fiscal history and fiscal performance to ensure efficient utilization of resources.
  • Performs other duties as required.

POSITION REQUIREMENTS

  • Proven knowledge of the Federal budget process including how budgetary legislation is enacted by Congress.
  • Proven knowledge of the DoD Planning, Programming, Budgeting, and Execution (PPBE) process.
  • Solid ability to consolidate various mission fiscal requirements into meaningful integrated programs and budget packages.
  • Experience in integrating complex legislative and higher headquarters policy and guidance to determine the effects on budget and programs.
  • Knowledge of federal accounting systems and their applications.
  • Solid knowledge of analytical techniques, practices, procedures, and principles.
  • Strong communication skills both verbal and written.
  • Solid experience in preparing briefings and recommend solutions to complete funding and resource issues.

TO APPLY
