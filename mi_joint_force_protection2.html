Leveraging AI to save lives, protect warfighters, and counter unpredictable threats.

Joint Force Protection (JFP) aims to use AI to transform the joint mission areas aligned against unpredictable threats. JFP advocates for the adoption of AI to save lives and make the Joint Force more resilient.

With our mission partners, JFP is bringing AI-enabled capabilities to:

  • Countering Weapons of Mass Destruction (CWMD) promotes AI readiness, identifies critical AI needs, and produces key AI products that improve the use of CWMD sensors and platforms. CWMD also pursues capability development projects with the explicit goal of converting key players within the CWMD enterprise into AI “early adopters.”
  • Humanitarian Assistance and Disaster Relief (HADR) leverages AI to reduce timelines associated with discovery, resource allocation, and enhanced situational awareness to improve disaster response and humanitarian aid operations.
  • Force Protection (FP) promotes AI maturity and adoption across the FP enterprise by identifying critical use cases and producing scalable pipelines and solutions that reduce operator burden and improve FP outcomes.

Joint Force Protection In Action

Project Gargoyle enhances situational awareness in security situations by using data from current force protection systems, such as CCTV, to develop computer vision technology identifying people and weapons faster than humans naturally can. This allows joint forces to prevent issues or mitigate issues more quickly. The tool is currently in testing with U.S. Air Force and U.S. Marine Corps.
