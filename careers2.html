Find Your Future at the JAIC

The JAIC was founded in the belief that advances in AI technology will rapidly change the character of warfare. The Department of Defense must rapidly adopt AI technology to field a military that can keep America safe, strong, and free in the 21st century.

From machine learning software development to program and project management, the JAIC is looking for world-class talent driven to tackle tough challenges at the intersection of national security and cutting-edge AI technology.

To Apply

Please submit a complete application package with the following documentation to JAIC Hiring Box at: osd.JAIC-Hiring@mail.mil

  • Cover Letter
  • Statement of Interest
  • Resume (required)
  • Unofficial transcripts (required)
  • DD214 (required if applicable)
Complete application packages must be submitted by 11:59 pm Eastern Standard Time (EST) on the closing date of the announcement. Incomplete packages may be removed from consideration.

If you have questions or concerns, please contact Amber Evans or Zana Abusakran at osd.JAIC-Hiring@mail.mil.
