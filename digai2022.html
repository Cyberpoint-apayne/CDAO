Announcing the 2022 DoD Digital and Artificial Intelligence Symposium

June 7-8, 2022

The 2022 DoD Digital and AI Symposium will virtually bring together thought leaders from across the national security enterprise to discuss themes critical to accelerating DoD’s adoption of data, analytics, and AI to generate decision advantage.


ABOUT THE EVENT

As with the 2020 and 2021 DoD AI Symposiums, this virtual event will connect professionals from Service, Agency, and OSD organizations that are driving the digital transformation of the DoD with innovators from industry, academia, and non-profit research institutions for two days of dynamic, insightful and broad-ranging speakers and discussions. During this time, there will also be an Exposition area for industry, academia, and government organizations to highlight their AI capabilities to the more than 2000 attendees.
