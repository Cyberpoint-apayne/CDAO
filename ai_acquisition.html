AI Acquisition

Rapid Done Right

AI and AI-enabling technologies are advancing at rates unprecedented in history. The Department must be able to access these technologies as quickly as possible - at the speed of AI. Therefore, the JAIC is focused on developing efficient AI acquisition processes, that foster a collaborative ecosystem, with academic and industry partners. To answer this need, the JAIC offers an expanding suite of innovative, decentralized procurement vehicles that enable rapid, ethical and responsible purchasing and delivery of key AI services and enabling tools.

Each vehicle is designed to address a specific AI-related challenge the DoD faces and deliver AI capability to the Warfighter through active partnerships between government, academia and industry.

Available Vehicles:

AI – DELIVERY through the Tradewind OTA:
To harness American innovation in AI, the DoD must quickly and repeatedly identify, acquire, and operationalize critical AI technologies from traditional and non-traditional companies. “Tradewind” provides market research through easy-to-use collaboration tools, facilitates Other Transaction Authority (OTA) Agreements, provides business process that supports the acquisition cycle - from ideation to transition, and facilitates teaming arrangements built on the collective expertise of industry, academic, and DoD partners.

For more information or to register for Tradewind: www.tradewindai.com
or e-mail the Indiana Innovation Institute (IN3) at Tradewind@in3indiana.com
Contact Us: osd.pentagon.dod-cio.mbx.jaic-tradewind@mail.mil

AI – DEMO TESTING via “Try AI”:
As AI requirements become more and more complex, there is an increasing demand for a “try-before-buy” capability for “demo testing” of existing AI solutions. “TryAI” uses a merit-based Commercial Solutions Opening (CSO) approach to order demos of innovative commercial solutions, leveraging funds or access to data to enable a more robust DoD-wide market intelligence capability.

Contact Us: osd.pentagon.dod-cio.mbx.jaic-acquisition-cso@mail.mil

AI – TALENT for AI Advisory and Assistance Support:
Highly-qualified AI advisory and assistance support (A&AS) is a scarce commodity. Many DoD organizations need assistance to help them craft AI requirements and build AI solutions. The AI A&AS Talent vehicle provides multiple-award BPAs to procure contractor support with the specific skills and backgrounds to achieve their AI goals.

Contact Us: osd.pentagon.dod-cio.mbx.jaic-acquisition-team@mail.mil

AI – DATA through DRAID (Coming Soon):
Many DoD organizations face challenges with the ability to access and use AI-Ready data. The “Data Readiness for AI Development” (DRAID) will offer multiple-award Basic Ordering Agreements (BOAs) for rapid ordering of data services (collection, cleansing, standardization, etc.) to address common DoD data issues and make DoD data “AI Ready”.

Contact Us: osd.pentagon.dod-cio.mbx.jaic-acquisition-team@mail.mil

AI – TEST AND EVALUATION (T&E) Blanket Purchase Agreements (Coming Soon):
The AI-T&E vehicle enables the DoD to rapidly order third-party T&E services that address the unique complexities of developing responsible AI solutions. Offering multiple-award Blanket Purchase Agreements (BPAs), this vehicle can be used to acquire conflict-of-interest-free T&E and independent verification & validation services.

Contact Us: osd.pentagon.dod-cio.mbx.jaic-acquisition-team@mail.mil
