Why the JAIC?

AI is changing the very character of warfare.

AI must be integrated across the DoD enterprise, operationalized across our missions, and trusted by those who use it do their jobs and the Americans we serve.

Along with our partners, the JAIC is accelerating the delivery of AI-enabled capabilities that will transform the Department’s efficiency and effectiveness, from the back-office to the front lines of the battlefield.
