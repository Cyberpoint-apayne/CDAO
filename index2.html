Why AI and What is the JAIC?

Artificial Intelligence is poised to change the character of warfare in this century. Our Soldiers, Sailors, Airmen, and Marines must be prepared to rapidly mobilize, deploy, fight and win in contested environments across every domain. Information is the strategic advantage that will enable our forces to orient, observe, decide and act faster than those that would do us harm. This is only possible through the rapid adoption of AI across the breadth and scope of DoD operations. The Joint Artificial Intelligence Center (JAIC)’s mission is to accelerate the delivery of AI-enabled capabilities and scale the Department-wide impact of AI, from the back-office to the front lines of the battlefield. [more, below],

Since its inception in 2018, the JAIC has partnered with industry, academia, and across the government, Services, Defense Agencies and Combatant Commands in areas such as business operations, military logistics, defense support to civilian authorities in disaster response, cyber, and health. Team JAIC’s primary role is to integrate the foundational cultural and technical elements across the Department to help harness the transformative power of trusted AI, including enterprise technical architectures, technical services, advisory services, policy reforms, AI governance, and workforce reforms.
